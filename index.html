Instruments of Power is a tactical RPG set in a grimdark fantasy world devoured by a spreading shadow. You are a sorcerer who made a world-shaking discovery — and in doing so, lost your body and unleashed an ancient evil upon the land.

Now bound to a mysterious sanctuary outside time, your disembodied soul survives only through magic. Freed from physical constraints, you can invoke multiple avatars of yourself — powerful, disposable extensions sent to reclaim what was lost. To fight back, you'll craft your own spells using discovered catalysts, effects, and modifiers, shaping your arsenal to match enemy tactics and manipulate the battlefield.

At the heart of it all lies a question: How far will you go to undo what you've done?

Key Features

  • Craft custom spells using discovered catalysts, effects, and mods
  • Invoke multiple avatars of yourself with full tactical capabilities
  • Two-layer gameplay: sanctuary management and turn-based tactical combat
  • Dark fantasy setting with a deceptive narrative
